Privacy Policy

Rally Africa takes the privacy and protection of your personal information very seriously. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is respected and your data is handled securely. This privacy policy note outlines how Rally Africa collects, stores, and uses your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit Rally Africa, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location. We may also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide you with a personalized experience on our website. We may use your email address to send you updates, news, and promotions related to African tourism. Your personal information may also be used for internal research and analysis to improve our services.

Information Sharing

Rally Africa does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing services to you.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure your personal information is protected from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption and secure protocols to safeguard your data during transmission.

Cookies and Tracking

Rally Africa may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how you interact with our website.

Updates to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy note may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

By using Rally Africa's website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this privacy policy note. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at [contact email].
