African Language Translators

Here are African language translator apps you can download for learning basic African languages:

- Google Translate

Supports translation for several African languages, including Afrikaans, Amharic, Hausa, Igbo, Somali, Oromo, Shona, Zulu, Swahili, and Yoruba.


- Microsoft Translator

Offers text and speech translation for Afrikaans, Amharic, Hausa, Igbo, Somali, Swahili and Yoruba.


- iTranslate

Supports translation for Afrikaans, Amharic, Hausa, Igbo, Somali, Swahili, Shona and Yoruba.


- Setswana Translator

Offers translation for Afrikaans, Amharic, Hausa, Igbo, Somali, Swahili, Twi and Yoruba.


- NKENNE

Translates text and speech for several African languages, including Hausa, Igbo, Yoruba, and Zulu.


- Anki

Can be used to create flashcards for learning African languages, including vocabulary and phrases.


- Memrise

Offers courses and user-generated content for several African languages, including Swahili, Yoruba, and Zulu.


Please note that while these apps can be helpful, they might not always provide perfect translations or account for nuances in language and culture.
